About

Everton de Matos is a Lead Security Research Engineer at Technology Innovation Institute, in the Cryptography Research Center.

He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio Grande do Sul (PUCRS), where he worked with the Embedded Systems Group under Prof. Fabiano Hessel.

He was awarded a Fulbright scholarship to develop his doctoral dissertation at the University of Southern California (USC), under the guidance of Prof. Bhaskar Krishnamachari.
